About Aara Farms

Company Overview

Established in 2018, Aara Farms has been Coimbatore's trusted name in quality poultry products. Located in the heart of Saravanampatti, we have built our reputation on delivering fresh, high-quality chicken, country chicken (Naatu Kozhi), and duck meat to our valued customers.

Mission Statement

To provide the freshest, highest quality poultry products while maintaining the highest standards of hygiene and customer service.

Why Choose Us

  • Over 6 years of experience in poultry retail
  • Consistently rated 4.8/5 by satisfied customers
  • Daily fresh supply directly from trusted farms
  • Strict quality control and hygiene protocols
  • Competitive wholesale and retail pricing
  • Convenient home delivery service
  • Deep understanding of local preferences

Location Advantage

Strategically located in Varathottam Bose Garden, Saravanampatti, we serve customers across Coimbatore with easy accessibility and prompt service.
